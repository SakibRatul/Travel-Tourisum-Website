Jaflong

Jaflong


Jaflong in Goainghat upazila of Sylhet. Its distance from Sylhet city is 62 km. It is located at the foothills of the Khasia-Jainta Hills in the Indian state of Meghalaya. When Jaflong stands at zero point, Dawki Bridge will fall in front. The Piane river flows below it. Mountains, rocky rivers, springs all together make the natural environment here attractive. Jaflong is called the daughter of nature! What makes Jaflong special is the lifestyle of the tribal people here. Khasiapunji is only after crossing Piane river. Punji means village in Khasia language. You can spend a day traveling to Jaflong with everything from the architecture of the Khasias' houses or the drinking barges there. Jaflong is not only a Sylhet division but also one of the most popular tourist destinations in Bangladesh. The nature here is similar to the famous rock city of Shillong Cherrapunji in India. The gateway to Shillong can be seen from the Dauki town of Jaflong.

How to go


If you want to go to Jaflong, you must first come to Sylhet, the country of tea from any part of the country. You can come to Sylhet by bus, train or plane. If you come by bus, buses to Sylhet leave at regular intervals from Syedabad, Mohakhali and Fakirapool bus stands in Dhaka. All major companies of the country including Unique, Shyamoli, Green Line, Saudia, Ena Paribahan and London Express have buses on the Sylhet route. Non AC bus fare on this route is Tk 450 to Tk 480. AC bus fare is 900 to 1200 taka. It takes 5 and a half to 7 hours to reach Sylhet from Dhaka by bus.

Dhaka to Sylhet by train


Dhaka Sylhet route has 4 intercity trains per day. Parabat Express leaves Kamalapur at 6:40 AM, Jayantika at 12:00 PM, Kalani at 4:00 PM and Upaban Express at 9:50 PM. Train fare will be from 265 taka to 1100 taka per person depending on the seat. It takes 7/8 hours to go to Sylhet from Dhaka by train. In that case it is convenient to go by Upaban Express at night. Sylhet can be reached by overnight journey.

Jaflong to Sylhet


You can go to Jaflong from Sylhet by Bus, CNG, Laguna or Microbus. Local buses leave from Shibganj bus stand in the city. The fare is 80 taka per person. Reserve 3500 to 5000 Tk if going by microbus. And if you take CNG, it will cost 1200 to 2000 taka. So the cost will be less as a group. Sylhet to Jaflong takes two to two and a half hours.

When to go to Jaflong


Travellers flock here all year round due to its natural beauty. In winter, the water in the river is low, so it can be crossed on foot. And there is a lot of current in the river during monsoon. Then the surrounding rocks, green hills, and magical fountains combined with Jaflong's appearance. So in a word it can be said that monsoon and its subsequent period is the perfect time to travel to Jaflong. That is, it will be better if you travel to Jaflong from June to November.

Nearby attractions


If you travel to Jaflong, you can visit several other attractions nearby. This includes Sangrampunji Mayari Jharna. Which can be reached after a 15 km walk across the Piane river. It mainly falls in the Indian part. But you can go up to a certain distance. Other places to visit include Khasia Punji, Tamabil Zero Point and Lalakhal. Apart from this, there is Sangrampunji Jharna, Panthumai Jharna near Bishatkandi. Lalakhal of Sari river, Bholaganj of Companyganj white stone. You can also go to Ratargul Swamp Forest and Bishatkandi.
