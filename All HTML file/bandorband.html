Bandarban

Bandarban


Bandarban is the most beautiful district of Bangladesh. If you want to touch the clouds in your Bandarban tour plan, like Nilgiri Nilachal here, you have to visit the mountain formations and mountain culture to see the Golden Temple, Megla Tourism Complex, Rock Falls, Chimbuk etc. Besides, there are many places for adventure lovers including Nafakhum, Amiakhum, Bagalek, Keokradong, Jadipai Jharna, Dim Pahar, Devtakum, Alikadam. It is said that if Travel to Bandarban is not on the list, the bucket list of a Bangladeshi tourist is incomplete! This district is called "Daughter of the Hills" because of its variety of natural forms.

Travel to Bandarban Plan


This Bandarban tour plan is mainly for those who want to visit Bandarban with family. Spots that are not suitable for families have been excluded. For those who are visiting Bandarban for the first time, or want to spend a couple of days in the cloud kingdom with family or friends, this travel plan will come in handy. You need at least 1 night and 2 days to travel in this tour plan. A perfect tour plan is very important when it comes to traveling. It saves time and costs less.

What to see in this tour

1st Day: Nilgiris, Chimbuk Hills, Shailprapat

2nd Day: Neelachal, Meghla Tourist Center, Golden Temple Bandarban You will have CNG, Land Cruiser Jeep as your vehicle. Or take Chander's car. A maximum of 4 people can sit in a CNG, 7 people in a Landcruiser and a maximum of 13 people in a Chander car. Determine the rotation vehicle according to the number of members.

First day


If you take a night bus from any part of the country, you will reach Bandarban early in the morning. If those who come from Chittagong leave early in the morning, they can stay in Bandarban by morning. After that, the first task will be to take the hotel. You can fix the hotel in advance, if you want, you can also come to Bandarban to get the hotel. After freshen up, leave for Nilgiris after breakfast. On the way you will see rock falls, Milanchari view point and Chimbuk Hill. You can stop the car while going to see them. Or you can directly go to Nilgiris. Will come to see on the way. The Nilgiris road will mesmerize you as it winds its way through the hills. New beauty at every turn. Return back to Bandarban city by evening. After freshening up at the hotel, you can spend the rest of the evening roaming around the city. If you want, you can go to Sangu Bridge and explore for a while. The town has various indigenous product shops. You can find various souvenirs in these shops to gift to your loved ones or someone. The distance from Bandarban city to Nilgiris is 50 km. It will take 4 to 5 hours to reach. It takes 7/8 hours if you add the travel time. So don't forget to look at the clock while roaming. To explore these spots, Sinji fares may range from Tk 2,000, Jeep fares may range from Tk 2,400 to Tk 3,000, and Chander car fares may range from Tk 3,500 to Tk 5,000. Bargain well when buying a car. Cars can be found at a slightly lower cost when excluding holidays.

Second day


Since you have been busy traveling all day, you can wake up early if you want. Then get fresh and have breakfast. On this day we will see Golden Temple, Meghla Tourist Complex and Nilachal. Each spot is within 5 km from the city. These places will rotate in two ways. Visit the Golden Temple in the morning, come to the hotel and take a rest. After noon, visit Meghla Tourist Center and Nilachal. Cloudy and bluish as well. Or go out around 12 noon first to Golden temple then lunch. Then go to Meghla tourist center. You can spend the afternoon in a pleasant environment in Nilachal. It will take 20 minutes to return to the city from Nilachal. Come back to the city in the evening. After dinner take a night bus back to your destination with some beautiful memories. Reserve CNG will cost you 800 to 1200 taka to visit these spots of Bandarban tour plan. Jeep will cost 1200 to 1600 taka and if you take Chander's car it will cost 2000 to 2500 taka.

How to go


Bandarban has buses from almost all the divisional cities of the country. All types of AC – Non AC buses go to Bandarban from Dhaka. Among the non AC you will find the buses of Shyamali, Saudia, Unique, Dolphin, Saint Martin, S Alam etc. Buses leave from Kalabagan, Fakirapool and Syedabad. The last bus leaves by eleven o'clock at night. Rent 580 to 620 Tk. AC bus fare is between 1000 to 1500 rupees. If you want to go by train, you have to board any train from Dhaka to Chittagong. Many trains leave Dhaka for Chittagong including Sonar Bangla Express, Suvarna Express, Turna Nishitha, Mahanagar and Godhuli. The fare is between Tk 200 to Tk 1000 depending on the train and seat. To go from Chittagong to Bandarban, you have to go to Bahaddarhat first. Two transport buses named 'Purvani' and 'Purbi' depart from there every 30 minutes. Rent is 220 rupees.

Best time to travel


Bandarban is a wonderful paradise of nature. Due to its beauty, tourists flock here throughout the year. Nature here changes form every season. Bandarban is different in every season. For those who want to see clouds and fresh nature, the ideal time to visit Bandarban is from the onset of monsoon to Hemant. ie from June to November. And for those who want to avoid the monsoon and see the view of Bandarban during the dry season, the best time is December to March. It is better not to plan Travel to Bandarban because these three months of April-May-June are very hot.
