Cox's Bazar

Cox's Bazar


Cox's Bazar is the most popular and largest tourist destination in Bangladesh. The world's longest beach is crowded with local and foreign tourists throughout the year. The wide sandy beach, the rows of beaches, the sun rising from the sea in the morning, disappearing into the waves in the evening. This enchanting form of nature will entrap you in the web of illusion. When talking about a trip with family or friends within the country, the name of Cox's Bazar beach comes to mind first. For various reasons, many people's Cox's Bazar tour is not 100% good because of a good guide. This article will help make your Cox's Bazar trip more lively. For Cox's Bazar corporate tour of your organization contact the country's most female friendly tour organizer Green Belt.

Best time to visit Cox's Bazar


Travellers flock here throughout the year to hear the roar of the waves attracted by the natural beauty. Cox's Bazar is more touristy in winter as everyone likes to travel in winter. But the sea is a place you can visit all year round. The sea changes its shape every season. As much as you'll love walking on the beach on a winter night, nothing compares to the soothing feeling you'll get after a downpour on a rainy evening. Again, sitting on the beach on a hot night of Hemant and seeing the beauty of the sea, it will be a beautiful memory of your life for a long time. But one thing should be kept in mind that traveling to Cox's Bazar from November to March will cost you 30% of what you will spend at other times of the year.

How to go


Dhaka to Cox's Bazar can be reached by road, rail and air. Among the buses going from Dhaka to Cox's Bazar are Saudia, S. Alam Mercedes Benz, Green Line, Hanif Enterprise, Shyamoli Paribahan, Sohag Paribahan, S. Alam Paribahan, Modern Line etc. Bus fares range from Tk 900 to Tk 2000 per seat depending on the class. If you want to travel to Cox's Bazar by train from Dhaka, you can travel by Sonar Bangla, Suborn Express, Turna-Nishitha, Mahanagar Prabhati/Godhuli, Chittagong Mail from Kamalapur or Airport Railway Station. After that, you will get different types and quality of buses like S Alam, Hanif, Unique etc. from New Bridge area of Chittagong or Dampara Bus Stand. The bus fare is 280 to 550 taka. Besides, several airlines including Bangladesh Biman, NovoAir, US Bangla operate direct flights from Dhaka to Cox's Bazar. Besides, you can come to Chittagong by air and go to Cox's Bazar by road as mentioned above.

Where to stay


Cox's Bazar has more than 500 hotels and motels of different quality ranging from five star hotels to cheap lodgings. The total capacity of these hotels is 1.5 to 2 lakh people. Among them, good quality hotels have a capacity of 50,000. So even if you do not book in off season, it is not right to travel to Cox's Bazar without booking a room in season. Apart from the cheapest hotels, the rest can be divided into three categories: premium, executive and standard. Premium Caterer hotels include Mermaid Beach Resort, Simon Beach Resort, Ocean Paradise, Long Beach Beach Resort, Sea-Gal, Sea Palace, Royal Tulip, Heritage, Cox Today etc. Their room rent will be between 6000 to 12000 thousand rupees. Executive category resorts include Hotel Sea Crown, Coral Reef, Nitol Resort, Beach View Resort, Islandia, Uni Resort etc. The rent per room will be between 3000 to 6000 rupees. There are many standard category resorts in Cox's Bazar. These include Sea World, St. Martin Resort, Honeymoon Resort, Ikra Beach Resort, White Beach Resort, Neelima Resort, etc. The rent of these hotels will be between 800 and 3000 rupees. During the off-season, hotel rental rates in Cox's Bazar are reduced by half. Big hotels also offer discounts at this time. If you reach Cox's Bazar during the off-season, you can find a good hotel at a low cost if you bargain for a hotel. One thing to avoid after reaching Cox's Bazar is to take an autorickshaw or a hotel on the advice of a CNGwala. Your hotel rent will definitely be higher. Because CNGwala takes commission from the hotel. Also, the further the hotel is located from the main beach, the lower the room rent. Every hotel in Cox's Bazar has a website and Facebook page. You can contact them if you want to book in advance. Those who go to Cox's Bazar with family, if the number of family members is large, they can also rent a flat There are two three four bedroom flats in Cox's Bazar. Depending on AC, Non AC and category, their rent will be from Tk 2500 to Tk 15000.
