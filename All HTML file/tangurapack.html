❑ Tour Plan Day 1:

After reaching Sunamganj, our Tanguare Haor journey will be done by Rupkatha houseboat. I will go to the watch tower first. A bird's-eye view of Tanguare Haor can be seen from this tower, which is submerged on all four sides. Here, the water will run on water. The next destination is the border village Teker Ghat. Here is the end of Howrah and the beginning of the Meghalaya Hills of India. At Tek Ghat we will visit the limestone lakes Niladri and Lakmachra. Stay at Tek Ghat at night.

Day 2: By 10 am we will take a boat to Barikka Tila, Shimul Bagan and the magical river that flows through the hills. After bathing in clear and cold water, I will return to Anwarpur in the evening. After dinner leave for Dhaka. Then I will come to Sunamganj and have dinner and leave for Dhaka by night bus.
