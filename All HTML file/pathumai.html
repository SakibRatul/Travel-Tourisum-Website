Panthumai Jharna

Panthumai Jharna


Panthumai is the name of a village bordering Meghalaya, India. Panthumai Jharna (Panthumai Waterfall) is named after this village. The village is located in West Jaflong of Goainghat upazila of Sylhet. Not only Jharna, this village is one of the most beautiful villages in Bangladesh. This fountain is surrounded by the mountains of Meghalaya, Piane river and beautiful natural environment. Panthumai Jharna is originally located in India. There it is known as Barahil Jharna or Borehill Jharna. Even in India, Bangladeshis can go up to a certain distance near this fountain with Noka. Boat rent 200 to 300 taka.

Best time to visit Panthumai Jharna


Sylhet is called the queen of beauty. Tourists rush to visit Sylhet all year round due to the inexhaustible pull of nature. But the real form here is seen in monsoon. Panthumai Jharna is also a must visit in monsoons. Then the fountain is full of youth. Clouds cling to the mountains and pure water tumbles in the Piane River. So in a word June to October is the best time to visit Panthumai Jharna. Boats are not available during the dry season.

How to go to Panthumai Jharna


If you want to go to Panthumai Jharna, you must first come to Sylhet from any part of the country. It is good to mention that Panthumai and Bishatkandi are located at a close distance. So Panthumai can travel around with Bishakandi for almost the same cost. Sylhet buses leave at regular intervals from Syedabad, Mohakhali and Fakirapool bus stands in Dhaka. All major companies of the country including Unique, Shyamoli, Green Line, Saudia, Ena Paribahan and London Express have buses on the Sylhet route. Non AC bus fare on this route is Tk 450 to Tk 480. AC bus fare is 900 to 1200 taka. It takes 5 and a half to 7 hours to reach Sylhet from Dhaka by bus.

Dhaka to Sylhet by train

Dhaka Sylhet route has 4 intercity trains per day. Parabat Express leaves Kamalapur at 6:40 AM, Jayantika at 12:00 PM, Kalani at 4:00 PM and Upaban Express at 9:50 PM. Train fare will be from 265 taka to 1100 taka per person depending on the seat. It takes 7/8 hours to go to Sylhet from Dhaka by train. In that case it is convenient to go by Upaban Express at night. Sylhet can be reached by overnight journey.

Chittagong to Sylhet

There are buses from Chittagong to Sylhet route of Saudia, BRTC and Ena Paribahan. Rent 700 to 750 Tk. Besides, Paharika and Udayan Express depart for Sylhet from Chittagong every day except Monday at 8:15 AM and Udayan Express at 9:45 PM every day except Saturday. 145 to 1200 taka depending on the seat category. It takes 9 to 11 hours to go to Sylhet from Chittagong by train.

Second day


Since you have been busy traveling all day, you can wake up early if you want. Then get fresh and have breakfast. On this day we will see Golden Temple, Meghla Tourist Complex and Nilachal. Each spot is within 5 km from the city. These places will rotate in two ways. Visit the Golden Temple in the morning, come to the hotel and take a rest. After noon, visit Meghla Tourist Center and Nilachal. Cloudy and bluish as well. Or go out around 12 noon first to Golden temple then lunch. Then go to Meghla tourist center. You can spend the afternoon in a pleasant environment in Nilachal. It will take 20 minutes to return to the city from Nilachal. Come back to the city in the evening. After dinner take a night bus back to your destination with some beautiful memories. Reserve CNG will cost you 800 to 1200 taka to visit these spots of Bandarban tour plan. Jeep will cost 1200 to 1600 taka and if you take Chander's car it will cost 2000 to 2500 taka.

Sylhet to Panthumai Jharna


To go to Panthumai first you need to go to Ambarkhana point in Sylhet city. There is a CNG station at the airport road. From this station local CNG goes to Haderpar Bazar. The fare is Tk 100-120 per person. If you want you can take full CNG reserve. In that case, the rent may be a little more than what is calculated as five people per head. If you want to reserve, you will get Laguna and Microbus rent in addition to CNG. After getting down to Hadarpar Bazar you will find boat ghat. From here book a boat together to go to Panthumai Jharna and Bishatkandi. It takes two to two and a half hours to reach Handarpar from Sylhet city. It will take the same time to return. So keep the long travel time in mind while planning the tour.

Boat Rentals


Reserve boat hire from Hadarpar Ghat is Rs 1550 fixed by local chairman and tourist police. 10 people can go in one boat. It will take about 40 minutes to go to Bichnakandi in these engine-driven boats. It takes an extra hour and a half to reach Panthuma. Most of the time the sailors demand extra money to go to panthumai along with the bed.
