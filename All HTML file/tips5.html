An efficient way to travel on a budget

Plan a group trip


When traveling anywhere, try to go in a group. It reduces the cost as well as increases the pleasure. There is also an added security. Suppose you go to Sajek or visit Lalakhal. A moon car or a boat will cost a lot to go there alone and it is also difficult to arrange a vehicle for one. On the other hand, if you take a group of four to eight ten people, the fare per person will come down a lot which is affordable for the pocket.

Travel in off-peak season

Be it domestic or abroad, wherever you go during its season or peak period the cost will be two to three times higher than other times. At other times you can get a very standard hotel room in Cox's Bazar between 500 to 700 taka, any long public holiday, any festival, end of the year and beginning of the new year, the room rent of the same hotel goes up to 1500 taka. During this time tourist centers are also very crowded and the prices of tickets and other things are also high. So if you travel during off-peak season or take advantage of personal holidays, the cost will be lower

Try to do it all yourself

Now there are many travel agencies around which arrange everything from booking bus, train, plane tickets to hotel booking, dining, domestic transportation to reduce the hassle. But it should be remembered that these agencies get commission from all hotels, transport companies for providing these services. So they will see their benefits more than your budget. In this case, the cost is greatly reduced if you do all the work yourself. If you find out and bargain yourself, in all cases some costs are reduced.

Pre Plan and Pre Booking

Before going somewhere, it is very important to study about the place and make a travel itinerary. As it has a good idea about the cost before the money is spent, it in turn reduces the cost. With the benefit of the internet, it is very easy to know about any place in advance, and if you have your own credit card, you can book hotels and tickets alone. If you do not have your own card, you can take the help of a reliable travel agency. TripAdvisor, Trivagod.com, MyTrip, Scanner are many other websites where hotels and vehicle companies offer various discounts throughout the year in terms of pre-booking. If you book from all these places in advance, it is possible to save money and get additional benefits. . If you book the ticket very close to the scheduled time of the journey, the price will be about 40% higher. Keeping these things in mind and planning the entire trip in advance can save a lot of money.

Carry dry food and water

Anywhere especially if you are going to a remote place, you must buy some dry food like crackers, chips, drinks like water, cold drink, fruit juice in advance and take it with you. These come in handy in times of emergency as well as save your pocket from the exorbitant prices of the local shops. At the trekking places or on top of the hill, local people have some shops where a bottle of water is sold for 30 to 40 taka and a can of coke for 100 taka. In this case, if you keep your own food with you, the cost will be reduced a lot.

Serving Community Tourism

Services provided by local people are called community tourism. Abroad and in some places in the country too, people host tourists in their own homes for a small fee. In this case, the money is almost half or less compared to the hotel. Sometimes in these places self-cooked food is available.

Hotel Laundry Service

Hotel laundry service like room service charges more than necessary. So it is better to keep a mini packet of laundry powder with you. If you absolutely need to wash clothes, do it yourself. Following the above rules is not a very difficult task. But it is possible to give another small tour with the money that will be saved by following these few rules. It is wise to travel cheaply rather than leaving the house empty-handed to explore the unknown.
