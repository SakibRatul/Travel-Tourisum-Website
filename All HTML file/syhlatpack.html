❑ Possible description of Sylhet trip

First day travel destination in Sylhet will be Bholaganj Sada Pathar. I will get down to Sylhet in the morning, freshen up at the hotel and after breakfast, I will leave for Bholaganj. There, I will soak myself in the transparent water of the white stone. Have lunch on the banks of Dhalai river. I will spend the afternoon on the stone bed of Bholaganj. I will return to Sylhet city in the evening.

After breakfast on the second day, I will leave for Jaflong. On the way I will see the Haripur gas field fire pit. From there lunch at Jaflong on the banks of Piane River. After visiting Jaflong and seeing the tea garden, we will go to Sangrampunji Jharna. Next destination is Lalakhal. After arriving in Sylhet in the evening, group based hotel room will be available to freshen up. After having dinner, I will say goodbye to Sylhet and board the bus to Dhaka.

⦿ Dhaka - Sylhet - Dhaka Bus Ticket. ⦿ 3 meals per day starting from breakfast on arrival in Sylhet and including dinner on the day of arrival. ⦿ 3 Star Quality Hotel. ⦿ Reserve boat hire at Bholaganj, Lalakhal and Jaflong.
