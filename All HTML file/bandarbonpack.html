❑ Travel to Bandarban planning

Leave on Thursday night and check in at the hotel on Friday morning at Bandarban. Freshen up and take some rest for breakfast. First I will go to Meghla tourism complex with new jeep, I will visit the whole complex. After lunch in the afternoon, after visiting Swarn temple, in the afternoon I will go to Nilachal to see the clouds and see Bandarban in bird's eye view. I will return to the hotel in the evening. Private shopping time after evening.

Saturday morning we will leave for the Nilgiris to get lost in the cloud kingdom. From there Chimbuk Hill. After lunch in the afternoon, I will spend the afternoon on a river cruise on the Sangu River. In the evening after freshening up in the group based room we will have dinner at 8 o'clock. Departure for Dhaka at 8:30.
