❑ Possible Description of Sikkim Travel

I will leave at night on the 1st day and reach Burimari border on the 2nd day in the morning. When the immigration customs opens at 9 am, after completing all the formalities, I will leave for Sikkim via Siliguri. Hotel check in at Gangtok before evening.

On the third day, after breakfast, I will leave for site seeing in the reserve jeep. Today we will explore Gangtok and its surrounding places of interest. Day 4 After breakfast check out from the hotel and leave for Lachung, the main attraction of Sikkim. We will stop the car on the way and see several frozen springs. Check-in to hotel in Lachung by afternoon. The rest of the day will be spent on our own in the ice kingdom around the hotel.

Fifth day after breakfast leave for Yamthang Valley and Zero Point. After spending two hours there in the ice kingdom, we will return to Lachung again. After lunch, I will leave for Gangtok. Overnight stay at Gangtok. On the sixth day, after breakfast, I will leave for Siliguri. I will come to Bangladesh border through Siliguri in the afternoon. Then cross the border and leave for Dhaka by evening bus. I will be in Dhaka at 5 am the next day.
