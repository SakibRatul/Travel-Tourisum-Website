Winter travel preparations and tips

Winter Travel Preparation and Tips


Winter is really the thing that tickles the mind of Bengalis besides pithapuli food is the desire to leave the house and go somewhere far away. The arrival of winter brings with it the message of the holidays as our lives embrace the natural harshness and dryness of nature. Besides, due to geographical reasons, the weather in our country is very suitable for traveling during this season, so it is a good idea to travel in winter to say goodbye to boredom. But not all places are equally suitable for traveling in winter, and winter travel requires a little extra care for the body as well as various accessories. If you want to leave the warmth of blankets and hot tea to travel in winter, let's know the details of all the things that need to be taken care of.

1. Appropriate clothing

The first thing everyone needs is proper winter clothing when going out from the comfort of the house in freezing cold. The quality and quantity of these clothes depends largely on where you are going to travel. Hills are best for walking in winter. And the cold is a bit more intense in the hilly areas than in other places. So take light weight warm clothes if you are going to the mountains or places with severe winters. If you are going to the sea or less cold places, a good quality winter clothing will do. Try to get warm clothes that are suitable for all conditions without adding weight to the backpack. Mufflers, socks, hand socks, hats can be taken in pairs as they get lost quickly. In terms of shoes, you can easily protect your feet from winter dust and cold if you take Keds, boots or sneakers instead of normal sandals.

2. Cosmetics

Even though the weather in winter is pleasant enough to walk around, the silent tyranny of nature continues on the skin like any other season. It is very important to always keep a small petroleum jelly or small portable container with cream, lotion, lip balm. And although the winter sun is very comfortable, it is equally harmful for the skin. So always use sunglasses and sunscreen when going out in the sun.

3. Inquiry

It is very important to know the detailed information about the destination before going on a trip. Traveling in winter is not recommended because the severity of winter varies from place to place. Therefore, before going out, you must check the weather there. Taking these little precautions will save you from minor hassles of packing or other preparations.

4. Booking

Winter comes with the mood of holidays so everyone likes to travel with friends and family at this time. Peak season for travel anywhere in our country means winter. So if you are planning to visit some far off popular places during this time, it is wise to book the vehicle and hotel tickets in advance. If not, in many cases, you have to spend a lot of money to arrange accommodation and transportation along with collecting extra money from your pocket.

5. medicine

Whether you travel alone or in a group, a slight headache is enough to spoil the joy of travel. Winter comes with various diseases including cold, fever, cough, so it is very important to keep cold, cough, headache, upset stomach and fever medicine in addition to first aid for travel.

6. food

Choosing food smartly by not eating too much oil or spices can reduce the torture on the body just as much as traveling in winter can be enjoyed. While traveling in winter, one should eat food that will keep the body warm and meet the water requirement. Chocolate, soup, stew, hot chocolate relax the body in cold. Fatty foods should be eaten outside the country where there is severe winter. Some more winter travel tips



Some more winter travel tips

Winter is the ideal time for trekking. Dry weather and lack of water make many trekking routes impassable at this time. As a result new places of travel are discovered. But in case of trekking in remote areas in winter, one should always carry light weight luggage, fire starter, cold proof sleeping bag.

Carry a portable water bottle. Water consumption is less in winter but the body can deteriorate anytime during the journey due to lack of water. A portable bottle can be refilled at any time from a reliable place outside the hotel or outside. Extra care should be taken when traveling with children in winter. Always take a little more children's clothes than adults and be careful to adapt children in cold weather. It is very important to get a flu shot before traveling to a place with severe winters outside the country. Because often there are various diseases from cold allergies to fever, cough, etc. can be canceled.
