Essential travel gadgets

Portable phone charger or power bank


Due to smartphones and the internet, we are constantly busy using various social websites and applications. As a result, no matter how many phone companies guarantee long life battery, we often have to face the problem of phone charging while traveling. But then maybe there is a problem to google something important or even to communicate with your people. It is important to have a good quality portable charger with you to avoid all these problems. Portable chargers of various quality and price are available in the market. But if you want to buy all these power banks or portable chargers, make sure about its capacity.

Power Shot Compact Camera

Those who don't want to travel around with heavy lenses, tripods, heavy body dslr cameras but are addicted to taking pictures. But you can travel with a compact camera. Brands like Canon, Sony etc. have several powershots with ultra slim designs. These cameras provide high quality images and are also convenient to transport.

Travel Drone

These days many people make travel videos and upload them on YouTube as a hobby. Travel drones create amazing opportunities to get bird's-eye videos to turn your wanderlust into a career through YouTube. Besides, many people travel with drones as a hobby. What is the harm in having a bird's eye view of the desired travel destination?

Universal Plug Adapter

When you go somewhere, you may carry various electronic devices like cameras, phones, ebook readers. If necessary, they have to be charged. Again, the pin of the charging port varies from place to place. So it is wise to carry only one universal all-in-one adapter instead of carrying different types of charging adapters.

tab

Those who travel abroad for official work or cannot escape from work while traveling can take a tablet instead of carrying a heavy gadget like a laptop. iPad or Microsoft's Surface 10-inch screen are lightweight tablets that are great for travel. Besides, many other companies sell high capacity and light weight tablets just for travel.

Noise Canceling Headphones/Soundport

Difficult to do any other necessary work while talking on the phone while traveling or outside. Having a pair of wireless noise-canceling headphones makes it easy to carry around while talking on the phone, as well as listening to music or other entertainment. Besides, since the headphones are wireless, there is no additional hassle of entanglement.

Water Purification Portable Bottle

When you go on a trip, instead of carrying a plastic bottle, you can easily get clean water by taking a water purifier portable bottle. And since portable bottles can be used again and again, it is possible to avoid environmental damage by throwing plastic bottles everywhere. Also, various devices are being invented day by day including mirrorless dslr cameras, selfie sticks, smart watches, smart binoculars, lenskits for phones which are making our travel experience more enjoyable. Using solar energy, various gadgets including solar rechargeable batteries, inflammable paper are making even the most difficult journeys like camping, trucking easier. These gadgets can be bought from any part of the world by checking online. In addition to the online platform Amazon, Alibaba, E-Bay, Esmos travel kit is available to buy in many big and small shops of IDB, Bashundhara City, Jamuna Future Park in the capital Dhaka.
