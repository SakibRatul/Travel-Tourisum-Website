❑ Possible description of Darjeeling trip

I will leave at night on the first day and reach Burimari border on the second day in the morning. When the immigration customs opens at 9 am, after completing all the formalities, I will leave for Darjeeling via Siliguri.

Third day after breakfast in reserve jeep we will visit Himalayan Mountaineering Institute, Rock Garden, Zoological Park, Ropeway, Tenzing Rock, Tea Garden, Japanese Temple one by one. On the fourth day, I will leave the hotel by four in the morning and go to Tiger Hill. From there I will see the sunrise on the 3rd highest mountain in the world, Kanchenjunga. Then I will see Batasia Loop, Ghum Monastery, Ghum Station one by one. On this day, starting from four o'clock in the morning, our tour will end within ten thirty o'clock in the morning. After that the rest of the day is personal time. Anyone can shop if they want.

On the fifth day, after breakfast, I will leave for Mirik. Enjoying the beauty of the hill town of Mirik at the foot of the Himalayas, we will reach Siliguri in the afternoon. After lunch in Siliguri, I will leave for Bangladesh. After completing the customs immigration in the evening, I will return to Dhaka by night bus. I will be in Dhaka at five in the morning on the sixth day.
