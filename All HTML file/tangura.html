Tanguare Haor(Tanguar Haor)

Tanguare Haor


Tanguare Haor is spread over an area of about 100 square kilometers in Sunamganj district. Only water and water as far as the eye can see. Many villages are submerged in it. Boats are the means of transportation from one house to another in monsoon. At one end of Haor are the mountains of Meghalaya, 30 small springs coming down from there and the blue sky on the horizon. The area of Tanguare Haor of seven thousand acres triples in monsoon. There are similar places to visit in Bangladesh anywhere else in South Asia. But there is nowhere else as diverse as Tangua Haor. So if a foreign tourist wants to know the name of the sightseeing place in Bangladesh, travel experts suggest the name of Tanguar Haor.
